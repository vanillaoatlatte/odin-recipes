Sage Vanilla Latte

Description

Earthy and sweet, the sage vanilla latte is a unique twist on the classic vanilla latte.

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Espresso
  • Vanilla syrup
  • Sage syrup

Steps

  1. Prepare your shot of espresso.
  2. In an appropriately-sized cup, dose 18g of vanilla syrup and 23g of sage syrup. You can adjust the sweetness, if desired.
  3. Add the espresso to the syrup, and stir.
  4. Top with steamed milk.
  5. Alternatively, add cold milk and ice.
