Cookie Butter Latte

Description

A Coffee Bar favorite, the cookie butter latte is sweet, creamy, and decadent. Ideal for when you want the warm taste of cookie butter on toast, but need something drinkable.

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Espresso
  • Cookie butter
  • Brown sugar

Steps

  1. Prepare your shot of espresso.
  2. In an appropriately-sized cup, dose 35g of cookie butter. Add 15g of brown sugar. You can adjust the sweetness, if desired.
  3. Add the espresso to the mixture, and stir.
  4. Top with steamed milk.
  5. Alternatively, add cold milk and ice.
