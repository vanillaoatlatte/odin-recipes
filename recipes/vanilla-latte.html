Vanilla Latte

Description

A time-tested beverage, the vanilla latte is an easy and accessible coffee drink for those who would like to drink a latte but fear the expected bitterness that comes with unsweetened espresso.

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Espresso
  • Vanilla syrup

Steps

  1. Prepare your shot of espresso.
  2. In an appropriately-sized cup, dose 30g of vanilla syrup. You can adjust the sweetness, if desired.
  3. Add the espresso to the syrup, and stir.
  4. Top with steamed milk.
  5. Alternatively, add cold milk and ice.
